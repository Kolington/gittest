About Animals Catalogs

Animal Catalog is a blog that post information about animals that have existed in this world in the past (Extincted) , are still existing and those that are stilling existing but at the point of Extinction. We create awareness for animals to be protected from Hunters, Poachers e.t.c. Animals Catalog work closely with the Official Wildfire Agencies and Non Governmental Organizations (NGOs) to protect animals right and lives from being extinct.
Animals Catalog has been saving animal lives from 2010 and are still saving more animal lives protecting them by putting them in their natural habitat. We have saved over 1000 animal lives working with Zoos,
Check our blog website for more animals at animalscatalog.com Visit and be informed.
